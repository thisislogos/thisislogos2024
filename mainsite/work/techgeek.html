Tech Geek

Developing a child's life skills through technology

Tech Geek's goal is to reignite a child's passion for learning. Believing that children are born scientists, curious, and eager to learn and explore — Tech Geek hopes to inspire children to stay curious and be life-long learners.

To achieve the clean, vibrant and slightly playful brand identity for Tech Geek, a vivid colour palette was carefully chosen to complement its playful take on a tech-inspired logotype.
