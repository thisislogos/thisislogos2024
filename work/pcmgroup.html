PCM Group

Identity redesign for a leading automative dealership in Malaysia

PCM Group, an established automotive dealership specializing in Toyota and Lexus cars, and also Topmark-certified used cars, embarked on a rebranding initiative to revitalize its brand image and align it with its evolving business objectives.

Lógos was engaged to lead the identity design phase of this rebrand. The project resulted in a refreshed and modernized brand identity that aims to effectively communicate the company's values and aspirations.
