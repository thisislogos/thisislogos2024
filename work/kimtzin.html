KIM TZIN

Mood and values

On the face of it, the assignment seemed simple: KIM TZIN wished to communicate efficiency & creativity.

It required the brand emphasise shrewdness in solutions and deadliness in outcome. The 'chef's knife' was the imagery offered.

Yet, the partners demanded that efficiency must be achieved without compromising on beauty.

From that, the contrasting sans serif serif typeface was conceived.
