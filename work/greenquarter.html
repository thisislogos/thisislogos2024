Refreshing Green Quarter's identity

Green Quarter's brand identity uses a simple, elegant and minimalistic design language. This is derived from the company's strong belief in remaining relevant in today’s ever-changing market by taking on new challenges while maintaining its minimalistic and efficient execution.
